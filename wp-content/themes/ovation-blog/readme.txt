== Description ==

Ovation Blog is a free theme designed specifically for writers and travel enthusiasts seeking to share their adventures online. With its immersive full-screen layout, stunning gallery options, and integrated maps feature, Wanderlust effortlessly captures the essence of your journeys. Whether you're a globetrotter, digital nomad, or weekend explorer, this theme offers the perfect platform to showcase your wanderlust-inspired content and inspire others to embark on their own adventures.. Even without any coding skills, you can easily setup and customize your site with this amazing theme.  The theme is minimal, luxurious, expert, elegant, sophisticated, clean, custom colors retina ready and responsive which can viewed on any device. If you are a blogger, vlogger or a video vlogger then this theme is for you. Also if you are into blogging, food blog, modern blog, travel blog, adventure blog, personal blog, social sharing, viral content making, 	artist blog, artwork blog, flower blog, herbal blog, masonry blog, music blog, personal blog, travel blogger, lifestyle blog, or story telling; this theme is your go to solution. It is a multipurpose and user- friendly theme with many customization and personalization options. It is also SEO friendly and translation ready theme. It has optimized codes, Footer Widgets, Custom Background etc. It is based on bootstrap framework. You can add a call to action button and link your social media pages to your website. Demo: https://trial.ovationthemes.com/ovation-blog-pro/
